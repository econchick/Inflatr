Welcome!

inflatR.com allows you to understand the inflation that we all face, but personalized for you. Not everyone experiences inflation the same way. A single, 30-something bachelor doesn't feel the same pinch of inflation as a retiree.

Guide yourself through the form below, select your respective demographics and estimation of monthly expenses. After the submission of the form, you will be presented with a graph that articulates a personalized inflation over time, compared to the US as well as your respective region. Feel free to play around to see how different elements affect your graph output.

DISCLAIMER: Your information is not being recorded.

Please note that this site is in beta, and therefore the estimation calculations, as well as the website application mechanics, are still rough around the edges (and maybe throughout).
